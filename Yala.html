Yala National park

Introduction

Yala National Park stands as a testament to the island's unparalleled biodiversity and natural beauty. Spanning over 97,000 hectares, this enchanting sanctuary beckons adventurers and wildlife enthusiasts alike, offering a glimpse into the untamed wilderness that defines Sri Lanka's ecological tapestry.Yala is a mosaic of diverse ecosystems, from dense forests and expansive grasslands to serene lakes and meandering rivers. The park's varied landscapes provide a habitat for an extraordinary range of flora and fauna, creating a haven for both endemic and migratory species.
