About Us

The Sri Lanka Wildlife Department is dedicated to the conservation and protection of our rich biodiversity. Established with a commitment to preserving the nation's natural heritage, we strive to create a harmonious coexistence between wildlife and humanity.

Our mission is to safeguard the diverse ecosystems that make Sri Lanka a biodiversity hotspot. Through education, conservation efforts, and sustainable practices, we aim to ensure a thriving future for both wildlife and generations to come
