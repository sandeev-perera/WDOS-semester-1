Nestled in the heart of the Indian Ocean, Sri Lanka unveils a spectacular showcase of biodiversity, making it a captivating destination for wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers alike. What You Will Discover

Elephants in their Element

Witness the gentle giants of Sri Lanka—the Asian elephants—in their natural habitats. The country is home to one of the highest elephant densities in Asia. Experience the awe-inspiring sight of these majestic creatures

Big Cat Encounters

For the wildlife enthusiast seeking the elusive, Sri Lanka offers a chance to spot the majestic Sri Lankan big cats

  • Leopards
  • Fishing Cat
  • Jungle Cat
  • Rusty Spotted Cat

Avian Extravaganza

Sri Lanka is a birdwatcher's paradise, with over 400 bird species recorded. From vibrant kingfishers to the rare Sri Lanka blue magpie, the island is a symphony of avian life. Don't forget your binoculars, as you explore the diverse bird sanctuaries and wetlands.
